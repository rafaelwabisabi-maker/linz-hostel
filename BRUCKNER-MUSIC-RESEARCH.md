# Anton Bruckner — Royalty-Free Music Research

> Created: 2026-03-19 | Purpose: Can we use Bruckner as background music for hostel videos?

---

## TL;DR: YES — compositions are public domain

Anton Bruckner died in 1896 (130 years ago). His **compositions** are fully in the **public domain** worldwide. No copyright issues with the music itself.

**However**: Specific *recordings* may still be under copyright. A 2024 Vienna Philharmonic recording of Bruckner's 7th Symphony is copyrighted. You need recordings that are also royalty-free.

---

## Why Bruckner Works for Linz Hostel

- Born in Ansfelden (15 km from Linz) — **LOCAL CONNECTION**
- Studied and worked at the Linz Old Cathedral (Alter Dom)
- Brucknerhaus concert hall is a Linz landmark
- 2024 was his 200th anniversary — cultural relevance is high
- His music = nature, grandeur, Upper Austria landscapes = perfect hostel branding
