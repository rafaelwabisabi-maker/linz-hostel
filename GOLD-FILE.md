## 5. THE DIFFERENTIATION — Teachcation & Secret Sauce

### Three Pillars
1. **Sleep at Anchor** — comfortable accommodation in a community-driven environment
2. **Move & Grow** — yoga, breathing work, flow arts, movement classes
3. **Teach & Learn** — residents and staff become teachers (workshops: sustainable living, entrepreneurship, etc.)

### Teachcation Initiative ("From Tourists to Makers")
- Strategic partnership with Grand Garage
- Guest journey: Arrive → Soft Landing (community + life skills) → Hard Skills (Grand Garage: machinery + technical expertise) → Creation (community project) → Departure (new skill + certification)
- Two products:
  - **Maker's Week** (B2C): 5 nights + 1 "Intro to Making" course. Targets off-season.
  - **The Residency** (B2B/Social Impact): long-term guests get discount rent in exchange for using skills to improve hostel
- Captures booming **Educational Tourism** market that standard accommodations can't serve
- Immersive courses: "How to Launch a Social Hostel," "Building Community-Driven Businesses"
