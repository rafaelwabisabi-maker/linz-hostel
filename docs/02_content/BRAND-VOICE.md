## Identity in One Line
A movement disguised as a hostel — proving that hospitality can heal a city's loneliness crisis.

## Audience (priority order)
1. **Investors** — Austrian angels, impact investors, local business network. Care about: returns, de-risking, social proof, clear numbers.
2. **Institutional partners** — JKU, Ars Electronica, Volkshilfe, Factory 300. Care about: mission alignment, visibility, concrete collaboration.
3. **Future guests** — International students, festival attendees, makers. Care about: experience, belonging, discovery.

## Tone
| Attribute | Means | NOT |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| Visionary | Specific future, not vague ideals | "We believe in community" |
| Data-backed | Real numbers anchor every claim | "Great potential" |
| Warm-industrial | Textured, human, grounded | Cold corporate, sterile |
| Confident | State facts, not hope | "We hope to..." / "Might be..." |
| FOMO-aware | Coalition is forming — are you in? | "Join us sometime" |

## Voice Rules
- **2nd person for journeys** — "You check in." Not "She arrived."
- **Name partners explicitly** — "JKU", "Ars Electronica", "Volkshilfe". Never "leading institutions".
- **Every post closes with coalition signal** — "JKU is in. Ars Electronica is in. Who's still deciding?"
- **Numbers over adjectives** — "90 beds, 3 revenue streams, Month 14 break-even" beats "profitable model".
- **Industrial texture in language** — words like: raw, built, running, live, operational, real, wired.
- **No startup clichés** — "disrupting", "ecosystem play", "leverage synergies", "at the intersection of".

## LinkedIn-Specific Rules (360 Brew 2026)
- Hook = first 210 chars. That line decides everything.
- 800–1000 chars per post sweet spot.
- 1 idea per paragraph. Max 2–3 sentences. Heavy line breaks.
- **No links in body** — 360 Brew penalizes. Put links in comments or Featured section.
- **No hashtags** — deprecated. 0-1 max if needed for brand.
- **ONE CTA per post** — specific action, not generic "follow for more".
- Bold sparingly — 1–2 phrases max.
- Post from: Daniel's personal LinkedIn (TBD: KraftWerk brand page).
- Post timing: Tue–Thu, 7–8am / 10–11am / 12–2pm. Max 3–4 posts/week, 24h+ gap.

## Anti-Patterns (never do)
- Generic hostel stock photos — TabakFabrik-specific imagery only
- Cold corporate blue — palette is warm/industrial
- "We're building something special" — tell them what specifically
- Posting with links in body — kills reach
- Warm-and-welcoming tone for partner posts — they need FOMO, not an invitation
