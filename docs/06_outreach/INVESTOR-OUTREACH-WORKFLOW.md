## Step 4: Discovery Meeting Prep

**Before every investor meeting:**
- [ ] Read their portfolio/investments (what have they funded before?)
- [ ] Identify overlap with Linz Hostel thesis
- [ ] Prepare 3 questions about their investment criteria
- [ ] Have Financial Model open (projections, break-even, ROI)
- [ ] Have pitch app loaded on phone/laptop
- [ ] Print Partnership One-Pager (backup)

**Meeting flow (30 min):**
1. **Story** (5 min) — TabakFabrik + vision + what makes this different
2. **Pitch App walkthrough** (10 min) — Interactive, not slides
3. **Financials** (5 min) — 3 scenarios, break-even at 60% occupancy
4. **The Ask** (5 min) — What we need, what they get, timeline
5. **Q&A** (5 min) — Their concerns, due diligence needs

---

## Step 5: Post-Meeting + Due Diligence

**Within 24h of meeting:**
- [ ] Send thank-you email + any promised materials
- [ ] Update CRM: Status, Notes, Naechste_Aktion
- [ ] If interested → send full package (BP + Financial Model + Partnership contracts)

**Due diligence checklist (have ready):**
- Business Plan v4.2.3
- Financial Model (3 scenarios)
- TabakFabrik location details / lease terms
- Partnership contracts (VantArc, etc.)
- Team bios
- Market analysis (hostel market Linz, occupancy benchmarks)
- Grant applications status (what's been applied for)

## Step 6: Term Sheet + Closing

**Investment structures to discuss:**
| Structure | Good For | Terms |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Equity share | Strategic investors | % ownership, board seat, exit terms |
| Convertible note | Angels/seed | Discount on next round, interest rate, cap |
| Revenue share | Hospitality operators | % of gross revenue for X years |
| Grant co-funding | Public money | Match funding, compliance requirements |
| Crowdfunding | Community | Fixed return or perks (free nights, naming rights) |
