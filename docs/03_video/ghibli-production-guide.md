# Ghibli-Style Video Production Guide — Linz Hostel

> Master reference for converting Tabakfabrik footage into Studio Ghibli aesthetic.
> Complements: `script-v1.md`, `shot-manifest.md`, `storyboard-v1/v2.md`, `nano-banana-prompts.md`

---

## Core Principle

> Use your REAL Tabakfabrik photos/video as the base, then style-convert into Ghibli — don't generate everything from scratch.

---

## Production Workflow (Recommended Order)

### Step 1 — Capture Base Footage (1-2 hrs on site)
- Phone is fine. 5-10 seconds per shot, stable.
- Static wide shots + slow walking shots.
- Prioritize late afternoon / golden hour lighting.
- Target 6-8 shots total (each 3-5 seconds):
  1. Exterior Tabakfabrik — arrival
  2. Entering / corridor
  3. Lounge "people soup" wide
  4. Teaching / yoga / learning room
  5. Kitchen / cooking / caring for the space
  6. Mentoring / cozy circle
  7. Night lounge, magical vibe
  8. Final call-to-action shot

### Step 2 — Ghibli Style Conversion (1-2 hrs + waiting)
Pick 3-5 hero clips. Use short clips (5-8 seconds) to stay in free tiers.

**Video-to-Ghibli tools**:
- Somake "Video to Ghibli Style" converter
- Other "Ghibli style converter" sites
- They handle colors, textures, outlines + temporal consistency (no flicker)

**If tool only does image-to-video**:
- Grab a still frame from footage
- Ghibli-style it as an image
- Animate as short zoom/pan (dreamy cutaway)

### Step 3 — Extra Shots from Single Frames (if needed)
- Take Ghibli-styled stills → Runway Gen-3 for camera moves / surreal transitions
- Great for "dream / concept art" sequences

### Step 4 — Edit/Clip with REAP or CapCut
- REAP auto-finds best moments, adds captions, cuts vertical reels
- Don't send raw footage to REAP first — do style conversion FIRST

## Scene-by-Scene Prompts

### Scene 1 — Student Arrival / Tabakfabrik Exterior
**Visual**: Exterior late afternoon, magical, birds, laundry on balconies, class banners. Student morphs subtly (skin, hair, clothes between cuts = diversity).
```
"International student entering a cozy hostel inside a red-brick factory, tall windows, soft sunset, Ghibli-style, character changing slightly between shots to show diversity."
```
**Line drawing variant**:
```
"line drawing sketch, international student arriving at industrial hostel, Tabakfabrik Linz, architecture coming alive, warm interior lights appearing, Ghibli atmosphere"
```

### Scene 2 — Lounge as Living Room / "People Soup"
**Visual**: Wide lounge — long table with laptops, sofa corner with books, guitar, yoga mats. Camera weaves through groups.
```
"Large industrial lounge with sofas, long tables, plants and string lights, full of diverse students and locals talking, eating and working, warm colors, Ghibli-style background, third-person view of one student moving through the crowd."
```
**Line drawing variant**:
```
"hostel lounge interior sketch, industrial hanging lights, diverse people gathering and talking, architectural line drawing style, warm beige tones, community forming"
```

### Scene 3 — Teaching Rooms (Yoga, Breathwork, Learning)
**Visual**: Bright classroom, wooden floor, mats, big windows with factory chimney views.
```
"Sunlit classroom in an old factory, wooden floor, yoga mats, plants by the windows, Ghibli-style soft light, diverse group doing breathwork, our student in the middle, calm and focused."
```

### Scene 4 — Partnerships (Factory 300, Ecosystem)
**Visual**: Quick montage — coworking glass walls, café sharing food, exchanging passes.
```
"Montage of an industrial campus: coworking space behind glass walls, café sharing food with hostel guests, people exchanging cards and smiling, Ghibli-style backgrounds, warm collaborative mood."
```
**Line drawing variant**:
```
"architectural blueprint of Tabakfabrik complex, Factory 300 connections, symbiotic partnerships visualized, sketch style, lines connecting buildings and people"
```

### Scene 5 — Working, Cooking, Caring for the Space
**Visual**: Shared kitchen, big pots, steam, laughter, handwritten recipes. Student in hostel T-shirt.
```
"Cozy shared kitchen inside an old factory, big pots, steam, people cooking together, warm light, handwritten notes on the wall, Ghibli-style, main student character helping and smiling."
```

### Scene 6 — Purpose-Built Lounge (Designed Social Tool)
**Visual**: Overhead/isometric showing zones: reading corner, noisy area, yoga nook, mini stage. Faint glowing connection lines between people.
```
"Large lounge in a brick factory hostel, divided into cozy zones, people connecting in each corner, soft glowing lines subtly linking them, Ghibli-style magical realism, evening indoor lighting."
```

### Scene 7 — Replicable Recipe / Student's Home Country
**Visual**: Transition from whiteboard sketch → different city outline. Student imagining a similar lounge (ghost overlay).
```
"Student standing in an empty room in their home city, imagining a cozy community lounge like the one in Linz, ghost-like Ghibli-style interior overlaid in front of them, hopeful expression."
```

### Scene 8 — Mentorship / "People Teaching People"
**Visual**: Small circle on cushions, tea, someone sharing a story. Hands helping with forms.
```
"Small group sitting in a cozy corner of a lounge, cushions and low table with tea, one older mentor speaking gently, others listening, Ghibli-style warm atmosphere, handwritten words 'people teaching people' on a board in the background."
```

### Scene 9 — Storytelling as Spell / Call to Action
**Visual**: Night lounge — fairy lights, dancing, studying. Words as glowing text dissolving into crowd.
```
"Night scene inside a lively hostel lounge in an old factory, fairy lights, people talking, dancing and studying, soft glowing words floating briefly in the air like a spell, Ghibli-style magical realism, final close-up of the student smiling at camera."
```

### Scene 10 — Call to Action
```
"welcoming hostel entrance, warm light spilling out, open doors, invitation to join, sketch aesthetic, community waiting inside, hopeful Ghibli atmosphere"
```

## Visual Tone Guide

- **Lighting**: Warm, soft, hazy, lots of small details (plants, posters, cups, books, cables)
- **Architecture**: Industrial bones (brick, concrete, tall windows, steel beams) softened with textiles, wood, hand-made signs, people
- **Camera**: Mostly 3rd-person following student (Tomb Raider / Ghibli adventure), sometimes over-the-shoulder
- **Color**: Warm terracotta, amber, golden tones. Cool blue for evening exterior contrast.
- **Line drawing style**: Beige and black ink, architectural, warm Ghibli lighting, parallax movement

## REAP Execution Workflow

### Option A — ADHD-Friendly (Fastest)
1. **Partner records** (15-30 min) — "Talk about the hostel vision naturally for 2-3 min. Phone, good light, clean audio."
2. **Upload to REAP** (15 min) — REAP auto-generates 3-5 clips, timed transcript, auto-captions
3. **Match B-roll** (30-45 min) — REAP highlights best moments → assign background scene to each

### Option B — Full Production
1. Film all segments (5-7 × 3-5 min)
2. REAP auto-edit → export individual clips
3. Background assignment per clip
4. Assembly in Premiere/CapCut or REAP timeline

### ADHD Speaker Cheat Sheet (send to partner)
```
Talk freely about:
1. Student arriving at Tabakfabrik hostel
2. Lounge as "living room" + people soup
3. Classes (yoga, learning skills)
4. Cooking/working together
5. People teaching people
6. "Spell of connection" vision
7. Call to join/support

Don't worry about order or timing.
Just talk naturally 2-3 minutes total.
```
