## What
A 90-bed hybrid hostel in Linz, Austria — adjacent to Tabakfabrik (repurposed tobacco factory / creative hub). Addresses Austria's loneliness crisis through hospitality innovation. Combines:
- **Commercial hospitality** (70% revenue) — short-stay tourism, dynamic pricing
- **Social Anchor** (30% revenue/stability) — long-term social rental + community lounge ("Flow Zone")

"Teachcation" concept: turning tourists into makers via Tabakfabrik ecosystem access.
